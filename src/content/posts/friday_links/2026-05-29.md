- [Bitmap fonts make computers feel like computers again](https://korigamik.dev/blog/bitmap_fonts/)

It was only in the last year or so that I started hearing people saying that any computer scientist or programmer should also study typography. I sort of jumped on the idea by purchasing a copy of Kate Brideau's [_The Typographic Medium_](https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262045858/the-typographic-medium/) when I saw it on the shelf at [Torn Light Records](https://www.tornlightrecords.com/). I've always found the subject to be interesting (go figure ...) and I think this article really articulates why. It's also (appropriately) beautifully formatted and an absolute pleasure to read. I also more recently stumbled upon [this](https://unsung.aresluna.org/a-few-interesting-modern-pixel-fonts/) as well.
